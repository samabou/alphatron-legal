1. Go to https://github.com and log in.
2. Create a new repository named: alphatron-legal
3. Upload all files from this folder (not the folder itself).
4. Commit the changes.
5. Go to "Settings > Pages", select:
   - Branch: main
   - Folder: / (root)
6. Save settings. After a few seconds, your site will be live at:

   https://yourusername.github.io/alphatron-legal/

You can now use these links inside your app or on Google Play.
